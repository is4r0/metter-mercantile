# Metter Mercantile — Design Brainstorm

## Three Design Approaches

<response>
<idea>
**Design Movement:** Appalachian Folk Revival — handmade, tactile, rooted in Southern craft traditions.

**Core Principles:**
1. Warmth over polish — every element should feel touched by human hands, not a machine.
2. Layered storytelling — the layout unfolds like walking through rooms of a mercantile.
3. Textured depth — linen, wood grain, and aged paper textures throughout.
4. Generous whitespace that breathes like a well-kept shop floor.

**Color Philosophy:**
Cream (#F5F0E8) as the base — the color of aged linen and old receipts. Sage green (#7A9E7E) for accents — the color of pressed herbs and Southern gardens. Warm walnut (#8B6347) for structural elements — the color of worn wood counters. Deep charcoal (#2C2C2C) for text — readable but not harsh.

**Layout Paradigm:**
Asymmetric editorial layout. Hero section is full-bleed with off-center text. Sections alternate between left-heavy and right-heavy compositions. No uniform grid — each section has its own personality, like different rooms in the store.

**Signature Elements:**
1. Thin horizontal rules with small diamond or cross motifs between sections.
2. Handwritten-style accent labels (using a script font) for section callouts.
3. Subtle linen texture overlay on background areas.

**Interaction Philosophy:**
Gentle, unhurried. Hover states reveal warmth — slight scale, soft shadow, color deepening. Scroll-triggered fade-ins that feel like turning a page. No aggressive animations.

**Animation:**
Fade-up entrance animations on scroll (0.4s ease-out). Parallax on hero background image. Testimonial cards slide in from alternating sides. Nav links underline draws in from left on hover.

**Typography System:**
- Display: "Playfair Display" — elegant serif with high contrast, evokes vintage signage.
- Body: "Lato" — clean, warm, highly readable.
- Accent: "Dancing Script" — for handwritten callouts and pull-quotes.
- Hierarchy: 60px display / 36px h2 / 24px h3 / 16px body / 13px caption.
</idea>

## Selected Approach: Appalachian Folk Revival

Chosen for its unique, handcrafted personality that perfectly matches Metter Mercantile's warmth, quilting heritage, and treasure-hunt experience. The asymmetric editorial layout, linen textures, and Playfair Display typography will create a site that feels genuinely Southern and artisanal — not like a generic e-commerce template.
